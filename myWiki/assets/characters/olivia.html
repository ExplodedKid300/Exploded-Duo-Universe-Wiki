Appearance

Olivia has short, messy brown hair, with the middle section dyed a vibrant neon green, giving her a bold and striking look. Her brown-orange eyes have a mischievous glint, reflecting her playful and clever nature. Olivia prefers comfort and practicality in her attire, wearing a neon blue crop top sweater with the shoulders cut off, paired with yellow sneakers and casual shorts. The outfit complements her laid-back yet confident personality, and she’s always ready for action, whether it’s a hacking challenge or an impromptu adventure with her friends.

Personality

Olivia is a free spirit with a playful, tomboyish attitude. She enjoys teasing her friends, especially with her quick wit and sarcastic humor. Though she can be mischievous, she’s loyal to those she cares about and will go to great lengths to help them. Olivia has a rebellious streak and enjoys bending the rules—though never with malicious intent—always preferring to operate outside the system. Her ability to approach situations with both humor and cleverness makes her a unique ally, and she’s never afraid to speak her mind or push boundaries when she feels it’s necessary.

Biography

Olivia is widely known as one of the best hackers in her age group, with an unrivaled knack for cracking systems and finding creative ways to manipulate digital environments. Her hacking abilities are so advanced that she can summon a holographic keyboard and hack in real life, altering systems and networks as she sees fit. However, Olivia isn’t interested in using her skills for personal gain, such as changing her bank account balance. Instead, she focuses on improving security and exposing vulnerabilities, always providing constructive feedback in the form of a friendly note explaining how the system owners can improve their security measures.

Her skills caught the attention of ExplodedKid and ExplodedGurl, and after discovering that they shared similar abilities, Olivia quickly became close friends with them. Their shared experiences with manipulating digital and physical realities created an immediate bond, and they’ve been inseparable ever since. As a grey hat hacker, Olivia operates in the moral gray area—helping others without breaking the law, but always finding her own way to challenge systems and structures she feels could be improved.

Abilities

Olivia has a unique ability to summon a holographic keyboard that allows her to hack into digital systems in the real world. With this, she can manipulate electronic devices, bypass security protocols, or alter data. While Olivia could easily manipulate financial systems or change her personal data, she chooses not to, as she believes in using her abilities for good. She is highly skilled at finding weaknesses in firewalls and security systems and instead of exploiting them for personal gain, she sends messages with helpful suggestions on how to improve security, all while leaving the system unharmed.

Her hacking isn’t just limited to computers—she can also control smart devices, adjust digital settings in real-time, and even manipulate virtual spaces. Olivia’s unique ability allows her to control the virtual world seamlessly, and she often uses her talents to help others out of tricky situations, whether by hacking a locked door or altering surveillance footage. She does all this while staying one step ahead of the authorities, using her skills for the greater good without ever crossing the line into criminal behavior.

Trivia

  • Olivia loves playing pranks on her friends, often using her hacking skills to leave them harmless yet hilarious surprises.
  • Her favorite pastime is uncovering hidden Easter eggs in games and software, and she gets a kick out of revealing secrets others have missed.
  • Despite her rebellious nature, Olivia has a strong moral compass and refuses to engage in any form of malicious hacking.
  • Olivia is a fan of old-school tech, and she enjoys collecting vintage computers and gadgets as a hobby.
